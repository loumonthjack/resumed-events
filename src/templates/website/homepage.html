Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main purpose of the QR codes at the events?

The QR codes serve to streamline the attendee experience. They can be used for a range of purposes such as quick check-ins, accessing event schedules, connecting with other attendees, and gathering further event-related information.

Can I generate a personalized QR code for my own event session or booth?

Yes, our platform allows exhibitors and session organizers to generate personalized QR codes for their specific activities or booths. This facilitates easier engagement and data collection for each session or exhibition space.

What kind of support do you offer if there are issues with the QR codes during the event?

If you encounter any issues with the QR codes during your event, we provide immediate technical support to address and resolve the matter. Our dedicated support team is on standby during event hours to assist you in real-time, ensuring minimal disruption to your event. Additionally, we offer comprehensive guides and troubleshooting resources on our website to help you navigate any potential challenges. Your successful event experience is our top priority, and we're committed to ensuring the optimal functioning of our QR code system throughout.

Do attendees require a specific app to scan these QR codes, or will any generic QR code scanner work?

No, attendees do not require a specific app to scan these QR codes. Any generic QR code scanner available on their smartphones will suffice. Simply open your preferred scanner and point it at the QR code to access the information.
